project information

bellefonte central railroad

client: personal project

description: this is a schematic, or subway-style, map of the bellefonte central railroad (BFC) network over time. the BFC provided freight and passenger service throughout parts of centre county, including bellefonte, state college, and the scotia mines. this map was submitted to the student map competition at the north american cartographic information society (NACIS) 2025 conference.

software used: adobe illustrator, QGIS
