project information

student discipline maps and visualizations

client: professors erica frankenberg (penn state university) and maithreyi gopalan (university of oregon)

description: maps created using data from the u.s. department of education's civil rights data collection (CRDC) to show disparities, especially racial disparities, in student discipline in u.s. public schools. this included cataloging states’ laws and regulations on corporal punishment use in public schools, corporal punishment and out-of-school suspension rates over time, and the spatial pattern of those practices’ use.

software used: adobe illustrator, arcgis pro, R
