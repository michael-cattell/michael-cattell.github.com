about me

i am a soon-to-be graduate of penn state university with degrees in geography and education policy. while at penn state, i have worked with a number of clients to tell stories and spatial patterns through creating maps, making data visualizations, and writing. my maps and writing have been published in academic journals, shown in classrooms, and used by local organizations around state college.

check out my portfolio page to see the various projects i've had the pleasure of working on, and visit the contact page to get in touch about potential project work!
